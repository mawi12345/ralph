## Teil B: Terme

<Exercise>

## Teil C: Gleichungen

### 11. Löse die Gleichungen.

<Exercise>

</Exercise>

<Exercise>
$$ 5x = 35 $$ <Solution>$$ x = 7 $$</Solution>
</Exercise>

<Exercise>
$$ \dfrac{x}{4} = 8 $$ <Solution>$$ x = 32 $$</Solution>
</Exercise>

### 12. Löse die Gleichungen und mache die Probe.

<Exercise>
$$ 4x + 3 = 19 $$ <Solution><br />$$ 4x = 16 $$<br />$$ x = 4 $$</Solution><br />Probe: <Solution>$$ 4 \cdot 4 + 3 = 16 + 3 = 19 $$ &#10003;</Solution>
</Exercise>

<Exercise>
$$ 3(x + 4) = 21 $$ <Solution><br />$$ 3x + 12 = 21 $$<br />$$ 3x = 9 $$<br />$$ x = 3 $$</Solution><br />Probe: <Solution>$$ 3 \cdot (3 + 4) = 3 \cdot 7 = 21 $$ &#10003;</Solution>
</Exercise>

### 13. Löse die Gleichungen mit der Variablen auf beiden Seiten. Mache die Probe.

<Exercise>
$$ 6x + 4 = 4x + 12 $$ <Solution><br />$$ 6x - 4x = 12 - 4 $$<br />$$ 2x = 8 $$<br />$$ x = 4 $$</Solution><br />Probe: <Solution>$$ 6 \cdot 4 + 4 = 24 + 4 = 28 $$<br />$$ 4 \cdot 4 + 12 = 16 + 12 = 28 $$ &#10003;</Solution>
</Exercise>

<Exercise>
$$ 5(x - 2) = 3(x + 2) $$ <Solution><br />$$ 5x - 10 = 3x + 6 $$<br />$$ 5x - 3x = 6 + 10 $$<br />$$ 2x = 16 $$<br />$$ x = 8 $$</Solution><br />Probe: <Solution>$$ 5 \cdot (8 - 2) = 5 \cdot 6 = 30 $$<br />$$ 3 \cdot (8 + 2) = 3 \cdot 10 = 30 $$ &#10003;</Solution>
</Exercise>

### 14. Löse die Gleichung mit Brüchen. Mache die Probe.

<Exercise>

</Exercise>
